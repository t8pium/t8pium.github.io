Feedback loops and constraints

A writing lane for tracing why outcomes happen through incentives, bottlenecks, loops, and second-order effects.

SYS
Mode
Systems notes
Focus
Cause and effect

Core question

What forces are shaping the outcome before anyone notices the outcome exists?

What this studies

  • Feedback loops that reward or punish behavior.
  • Constraints that decide what is realistically possible.
  • Hidden incentives inside groups, schools, platforms, and projects.
  • Second-order effects after the obvious effect.

Use

This is the writing mode for turning messy situations into maps instead of opinions.
