Tradeoffs and incentives

A writing lane for breaking decisions into pressure, incentives, ego, opportunity, risk, and consequence.

DEC
Mode
Reasoning notes
Focus
Tradeoffs and judgment

Core question

What choice makes sense once every option has a cost?

What this studies

  • How incentives reshape behavior.
  • Why people optimize for image instead of outcomes.
  • How short-term comfort creates long-term traps.
  • What a decision reveals about priorities.

Use

This format works for essays about strategy, competition, school, personal discipline, and character decisions.
