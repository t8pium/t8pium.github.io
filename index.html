01 / ABOUT

Code, hardware, and the thinking between them.

I’m a student in Dubai working between software, AI, robotics, and writing.

I like projects where code touches the physical world or turns messy information into something usable. That usually means a prototype on the desk, several broken assumptions, and a clearer system by the end.

I care about building real things, learning fast, and documenting the thinking clearly. The goal is not to look busy. It is to become difficult to ignore through the quality of the work.
